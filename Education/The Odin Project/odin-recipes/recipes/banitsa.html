Banitsa Recipes

Description

Banitsa is a traditional pastry dish made in Bulgaria.

Prepared by layering a mixture of whisked eggs, natural yogurt and pieces of white brined cheese between filo pastry and then baking it in an oven.

In terms of the taste, it could be either sweet or salty.

Ingredients

  • Peels for Banitsa, 1 pack(500g)
  • Oil, 150ml
  • Eggs, 5
  • Yoghurt, 400g
  • Baking powder, 10g
  • Feta cheese, 300g

Steps

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs, add the yogurt, crumbled cheese and baking powder, mix.
  2. Brush the peels with the oil two peels at a time.
  3. Pour 5 tablespoons of the mixture evenly on each pair of peels.
  4. Roll the peels into a rolls and place them in the tray.
  5. Place the tray in an oven preheated to 180Â°C and bake for 40-45 minutes until done.
