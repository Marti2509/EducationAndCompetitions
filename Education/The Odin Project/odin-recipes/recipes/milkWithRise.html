Milk with Rise Recipes

Description

Rice pudding is a dish made from rice mixed with water or milk and other ingredients such as cinnamon, vanilla and raisins.

Rice milk has a mild taste and is usually naturally sweet in flavour. Because of its minimal ingredients, rice milk has a watery consistency.

Ingredients

  • Rise, 200g
  • Milk, 400ml
  • Sugar, 120g

Steps

  1. Boil the rice until half cooked.
  2. Boil the rice until almost cooked with the milk.
  3. If you want you can put cinnamon, vanilla and raisins on top.
